ACROCRUISER

The ACROCRUISER is made of a wooden frame with some components of particularly stressed high-strength steel. The wings are cantilevered frame with front main spar and fake rear spar. The wings are equipped with ailerons and flaps differentiated double slit, the structure of the wings is made of plywood covered with canvas, the flaps have a similar structure with canvas lining. The vertical tail plane is completely covered in a single spar of okoume plywood for the fixed part, the movable part has a structure covered in canvas.
